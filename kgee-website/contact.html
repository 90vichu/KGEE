Frequently Asked Questions

KGEE 2026 will be held from November 19-21, 2026 at the exhibition venue in Kochi, Kerala. The exhibition runs from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM on all three days.

You can register to visit KGEE 2026 directly on our website. Fill in your details on the registration form, and you'll receive a confirmation email with your entry details. Registration is subject to verification.

We offer various stall sizes including Small (9 sqm), Medium (18 sqm), Large (27 sqm), and custom configurations. Each package includes prime exhibition space, marketing support, and complimentary passes. Contact us for detailed pricing and availability.

We can provide information on nearby hotels and accommodation options at various price points. Contact our team for recommendations and possible group booking arrangements.

Yes, KGEE 2026 welcomes international exhibitors and visitors. We can assist with visa information and logistics support. Please reach out to us for international participation details.

KGEE 2026 features a comprehensive showcase of renewable energy and clean tech sectors including Solar, Wind, Energy Storage, Electric Vehicles, Green Hydrogen, Smart Grid Technology, and emerging clean technologies. Visit our Industries page for complete details.
